Library Overview

The library staff are available to assist you with:

  • Learning how to use the library and all of its resources
  • Accessing library materials and resources from off campus
  • Choosing the best research tools
  • Searching the online catalogue to find books, periodicals, videos and selected websites
  • Planning a research strategy for finding information for essays, papers, projects and reports
  • Searching for and locating periodical articles within the Research Databases or in print
  • Effectively search websites
  • Critically evaluating information
  • Finding information beyond the college library
